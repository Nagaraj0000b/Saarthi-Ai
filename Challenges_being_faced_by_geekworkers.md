## Problem Identification and Proposed Solutions – GeekWorkers Platform

### 1. Excessive Working Hours

**Problem:**
Many geek workers work beyond safe weekly limits, leading to physical health issues, mental stress, burnout, and reduced long-term productivity.

**Solution:**
Geek workers are encouraged to follow controlled and sustainable working hours by clearly understanding how excessive work negatively affects their health, work quality, and long-term earnings. This promotes self-regulation instead of forced restrictions.

---

### 2. Financial Imbalance and Income Uncertainty

**Problem:**
Geek workers experience unstable and unpredictable income due to uneven job distribution and lack of reliable earning forecasts, which often results in overworking.

**Solution:**
Data-driven income planning using historical earning patterns provides realistic income expectations. This helps geek workers plan their work efficiently and reduces financial stress caused by income uncertainty.

---

### 3. Exploitation and Abuse of Women Geek Workers

**Problem:**
Women geek workers face harassment, abuse, and unsafe interactions with users, along with insufficient protection and delayed response mechanisms.

**Solution:**
Strong protection measures and strict action against abusive behavior ensure a safer working environment. This encourages women geek workers to report issues confidently without fear of negative consequences.

---

### 4. False Complaints and Unfair Account Blocking

**Problem:**
Geek workers are sometimes penalized or blocked based on false or unverified complaints, without proper investigation or an opportunity to present their side.

**Solution:**
A fair and transparent complaint-handling process ensures complaints are verified before action is taken. Geek workers are given a chance to explain their side, preventing misuse of the complaint system and ensuring fairness.
